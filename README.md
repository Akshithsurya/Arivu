*About · Palakkad, Kerala*

## Akshith Surya

Grade 10 CBSE student from Palakkad, Kerala. NASA Space Apps 2025 Global Nominee — team leader of RC_Real Coding. ISRO Space Week 1st Prize (project) and 2nd Prize (quiz). Mentored by Dr. Aswin Sekhar, planetary scientist at NASA, specializing in near-Earth objects and meteor dynamics.

I build AI-powered exoplanet detectors, hardware pentesting frameworks, and science career platforms. Usually all at the same time, usually at night, always in Palakkad.

Interested in transit photometry, near-Earth objects, dark matter, neutron stars, and anything the universe hasn't explained yet. Hardware security researcher who works with ESP32s, RF signals, and firmware that was never meant to be touched. Grounded in the Kerala mathematical tradition — the first calculus textbook was written here, in Malayalam, three hundred years before Leibniz.

Working toward a future in astrophysics and aerospace research. One commit at a time.

> *"One commit, one paper, one launch at a time."*
