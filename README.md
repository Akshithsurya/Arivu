# Arivu

**Career guidance for students in Palakkad, Kerala — built by a student, for students.**

Arivu (Malayalam: "knowledge") is an open, community-rooted platform that maps the full landscape of careers available to students in and around Palakkad. It addresses a real gap: most career resources online assume urban infrastructure, coaching access, and English fluency as defaults. Arivu does not.

---

## What it does

Arivu presents structured profiles across 42+ career paths — spanning science, engineering, arts, trades, law, medicine, education, public service, and emerging fields — with each profile written in plain language that a student with no prior exposure to the field can actually use.

Each career entry covers:

- What the work actually involves, day to day
- The educational route from Class 10 onward
- Entrance exams, certifications, and timelines
- Realistic salary ranges and growth trajectories
- Resources accessible from Kerala, including regional institutions

The platform is designed to function as a first conversation, not a final authority.

---

## Design

The current interface follows a "Kerala Chronicle" aesthetic — drawing on the visual language of aged Malayalam newspapers and vintage textbook printing. Sepia tones, deliberate typography, and a skeuomorphic layout are used to make the platform feel grounded and familiar rather than generic.

The design is intentional: a student in Palakkad should feel that this resource was made with them in mind, not adapted from something made for someone else.

---

## Project context

Arivu was built as a community project in Palakkad with a focus on practical reach. It is deployed on Netlify and developed as a static site.

Live site: [arivu-pallakad.netlify.app](https://arivu-pallakad.netlify.app)
